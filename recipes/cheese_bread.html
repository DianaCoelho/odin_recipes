Cheese Bread

    Ingredients:
  • 500g of cheese
  • 500g polvilho
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
      • Method of preparation
  • Mix all dry ingredients together
  • Add water until you get the right consistency
  • Roll the dough into small balls and put in the oven at 475F
  • When golden brown, remove from the oven and serve
